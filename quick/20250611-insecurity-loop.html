Insecurity Loop

If you get insecure from your own insecurity, you'll fail.

Same for:

  • Get anxious of being anxious.
  • Fear your own fears.
  • Get stressed of being stressed.

It's like microphony, it gets loud and loud… And then, you fuck with something.1

Don't let yourself enter the Loop from Hell.2
