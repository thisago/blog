#+title: Decision Entropy
#+filetags: decision entropy
#+include: _base.org

Every decision is another irrevocable byte in the entropy of your life.

Made decisions could not be undone, they're your past.

The only way to change the past is to make new decisions in the present.

But don't get fooled, waiting is also a decision.
